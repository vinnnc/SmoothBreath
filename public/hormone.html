Hormone and Asthma

Menstruation sexual asthma refers to women with menstrual cycles, early in the menstrual cycle or menstrual period the phenomenon of asthma symptoms aggravate, generally used in women of childbearing age 5 to 7 days before menstruation has obvious tendency of asthma, especially in the peak incidence of 2 ~ 3 days before menstruation, called "asthma" before menstruation, menstrual cramps symptoms after lifting. Some attacks during menstruation, called "menstrual asthma."

The main clinical features of patients with menstrual asthma are that the symptoms of bronchial asthma are asthma or aggravation during the premenstrual or menstrual period, and the symptoms of the respiratory tract are similar to other types of asthma.

Note: if you have asthma, be especially careful during this time. This is when progesterone "inflates" many aspects of the body, causing difficulty breathing and possibly exacerbating asthma. Women who suffer from asthma should consider taking contraceptives to control hormonal changes, according to a study. You can also take more anti-asthma medications in the week before your period.
