What are asthma triggers?

There are many sensitive things that are related to asthmatics compares to those people without asthma.


The things that can lead to asthma symptoms are called triggers. Most asthmatics have common triggers, like flu, dust mites, smoke, exercise and pollen. Patients living in remote areas of Victoria face the same problem. Vast areas of land are home to a wide variety of plants, including many pollens that can trigger asthma. The female menstrual cycle is also an important factor affecting women with asthma.

TUnderstanding the different triggers has long been a good way to prevent and relieve asthma symptoms.
