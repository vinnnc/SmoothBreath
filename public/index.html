Asthma Trigger

Based on our study, there are several triggers which are the mainly reason of your asthma attack.

Bushfire

Especially in the summer, bushfire would occur occasionally in rural Victoria. Do you know that it is one of the main trigger of asthma attack?

Hormone

Do you know that female period is also a trigger of asthma attack?



Pets

Pets are our best friends, they give us sense of happiness and always stand on your side. However, do you know that they may also trigger your asthma?

Pollen

Asthma attack is more happened during the pollen season, since there are a range of pollen sources exist in rural Victoria.
