Rural Victoria Asthma Challenge

We provide service to asthma females in rural areas.
You can find out a customized plan and advice with your situation.
You will experience a better life with the proper control of asthma disease.

Did you know that 25% of all Asthma deaths in Australian occurred in Victoria.

Did you know that women who live in rural Victoria have a 6% higher occurrence of Asthma than the one who lived in urban areas.

Approximately 20% to 30% of people in rural areas have had at least one asthma attack.

More than 28 billion dollars is spent on Asthma in Australia.

Asthma Trigger

Based on our study, there are several triggers which can bring on or worsen your asthma attack.

Bushfire

In the summer, bushfire would appear more frequently in rural Victoria. Do you know that it is one of the main triggers of asthma attacks?

Hormone

Do you know that the female period is also a trigger of an asthma attack?



Pets

Pets are our best friends, they give us a sense of happiness and always stand on our side. However, do you know that they may also trigger your asthma?

Pollen

An asthma attack more happens during the pollen season since there is a range of pollen sources that exist in rural Victoria.
