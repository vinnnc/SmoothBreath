What we have researched in Asthma

The general visualization of asthma disease can be found below.
This information is shown through bar chart, pie chart and severity of asthma.

Rural and Urban Area Female Age Distributions

"From the chart, the rural area has higher asthma patient"

As one of the chronic diseases, many females have asthma. However, the incidence of asthma is varying from different locations and age groups. The bar chart shows the incidence of female asthmatic in both rural Victoria area and urban Victoria. The red bar represents Rural area whereas the dark blue bar is for the urban. In all age groups, the incidence of asthma in the rural area are higher than urban, especially in the age of 25 to 34.

Asthma Incidence in Different Age Group of Female People

"From the chart, female people whose age is 25 to 34 have the highest number."

In Rural Victoria area, there is approximately 11,000 female adult asthmatic who is under 65 years old. In age 25 to 34, there are almost 30,000 patients. Followed by females aged in 45 to 54 years old, which have around 20,000 patients. Compared to these two age groups, the people aged in 18 to 24 years old seems to have the least incidence of asthma.
