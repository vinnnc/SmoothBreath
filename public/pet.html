Pets and Asthma

Female people prefer to keep a pet with them. Maybe you don't know? Pets as our good friends, but their dandruff is another cause of asthma. We have to say, having a lovely pet in a Rural Victoria area with you can bring a lot of joy to your life, but as an asthmatic, you may need to be careful about your pets. Different species of animal have different amounts of hair loss, and it is very dangerous. In order to make the residents of rural areas of Victoria realize the seriousness of the problem and want to have their own pets, we provide the ranking of pet hair loss. People can use the following ranking to ensure that they are not getting worse and still have a playmate in life. Our website also provides some basic knowledge of daily life with pets to help asthma sufferers live better with their pets.

How to prevent asthma caused by pets?

  • Try not to let pets into the bedroom.
  • Try not to let pet hair stained furniture.
  • Vacuum your room regularly to keep it clean.
  • Groom your pet regularly (preferably outdoors) to reduce the amount of hair shed indoors.
  • Bathe your pet regularly.
