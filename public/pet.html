Pets and Asthma

We love our pets, but while they bring pleasure, they can also cause asthma with their hair and dander. Different types of animals have different amounts of hair loss, and pets with high hair loss are not friendly to asthma sufferers.

We recommend pets that have minimal hair loss for asthmatic. Check out our gallery below for an idea on Cats and Dogs with different hair loss.

How to prevent asthma caused by pets?

  •   Avoid pets into the bedroom.
  •   Keep furniture free from pet hair.
  •   Vacuum your home regularly to keep it clean.
  •   Groom your pet regularly (preferably outdoors) to reduce the amount of hair shed indoors.
  •   Bathe your pet regularly.
